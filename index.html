Privacy Policy

EMV Card Studio

Effective: February 12, 2026 Developer: Kemet Soft Version: 1.0.0

1. Introduction

EMV Card Studio ("the App") is developed by Kemet Soft ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our App.

EMV Card Studio is an educational and professional tool for reading and analyzing EMV contactless payment cards via NFC technology. The App is designed to operate entirely offline with no data leaving your device.

Key Principles

  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • The App has no internet connectivity
  • We do not collect, transmit, or share any user data
  • You have complete control over your data at all times

2. Information Stored on Your Device

2.1 Card Data

When you scan an EMV payment card, the App reads and stores the following information locally on your device:

Card Information:
  • Primary Account Number (PAN)
  • Expiration date
  • Cardholder name (if present on card)
  • Card type (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, etc.)
  • Application Identifiers (AIDs)
Technical Data:
  • APDU commands and responses exchanged with the card
  • EMV tag-value pairs (TLV data)
  • Application Transaction Counter (ATC)
  • Card verification results
  • Application File Locator data

Storage: All card data is stored in an encrypted database on your device using SQLCipher with AES-256 encryption.

2.2 Data NOT Collected

The App does not and cannot access:

  • CVV/CVC security code (not accessible via contactless NFC)
  • PIN (not accessible via contactless NFC)
  • Full magnetic stripe track data
  • Sensitive Authentication Data (SAD) as defined by PCI-DSS

2.3 App Preferences

The App stores your preferences locally on your device:

  • PAN masking level setting
  • Biometric authentication preference
  • Screenshot protection preference
  • Duplicate card handling preference
  • Clipboard auto-clear preference
  • In-app search history

2.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (email, phone number, address)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, advertising ID)
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • Contacts, photos, or media

3. How Your Data is Used

Data stored on your device is used solely to provide the App's core functionality:

  • Display card information within the App
  • Visualize APDU command/response exchanges
  • Compare data across multiple scanned cards
  • Search and organize your scanned cards
  • Generate export files for your personal use
  • Show charts and statistics about your scanned cards

Your data is never used for analytics, advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the features described above.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Encryption

Database Encryption:
  • Algorithm: AES-256 via SQLCipher 4.13.0
  • Passphrase: 256-bit randomly generated, stored in Android EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • Protection: Android application sandbox with optional biometric authentication
Runtime Encryption:
  • Algorithm: AES-256-GCM via Android Keystore
  • Key storage: Hardware-backed when available (Trusted Execution Environment / Secure Element)

4.2 Additional Security Measures

  • PAN Masking: Four configurable levels (Full display, Last 4 digits, First 6 + Last 4, Fully masked)
  • Biometric Authentication: Optional fingerprint or face unlock to access the App
  • Screenshot Protection: FLAG_SECURE prevents screen capture on sensitive screens
  • Clipboard Security: Automatic clipboard clearing after 30 seconds
  • Code Obfuscation: ProGuard/R8 enabled for release builds

4.3 No Data Transmission

The App does not transmit any data over the internet or any other network:

  • No INTERNET permission is declared in the App
  • No HTTP/HTTPS libraries are included
  • No backend servers or APIs exist
  • No cloud storage or synchronization
  • The App functions entirely offline

NFC communication occurs only between your device and a physical card held within 4 cm range, using the ISO 14443-4 protocol. This is a local, short-range communication and does not involve the internet.

4.4 Backups

Cloud backups are disabled (android:allowBackup="false") to prevent sensitive data from being included in Android system backups or Google Drive auto-backup.

You may optionally export card data to a password-encrypted file stored at a location of your choosing. Exported files are under your control.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data. We do not have access to your data.

  • No data is sent to Kemet Soft or any third party
  • No analytics providers are used (no Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks are integrated
  • No social media services are connected
  • No backend servers receive your data

Since all data remains on your device and we have no means to access it, we cannot provide your data to anyone, including in response to legal requests.

6. Third-Party Libraries

The App uses the following open-source libraries for technical functionality:

7. Your Rights and Control

7.1 Access

You have complete access to all data stored by the App through its interface. You can view, search, and browse all scanned cards and their associated data at any time.

7.2 Deletion

You can delete your data at any time using the following methods:

  • Delete individual cards: Long-press a card and select Delete
  • Clear all data: Settings > Clear All Data
  • Uninstall the App: Removes all App data from your device

Deletion is immediate and permanent. No data is retained after deletion.

7.3 Data Portability

You can export your data for personal use:

  • Export to encrypted JSON files
  • Export to HTML reports
  • Files are saved to a location you choose and are under your control

7.4 Modification

You can edit card metadata including categories, favorites, titles, and descriptions within the App.

8. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

Permission Purpose Required
NFC Read EMV card data via Near Field Communication Yes (core functionality)
VIBRATE Provide haptic feedback when a card is detected Yes (user experience)
USE_BIOMETRIC Optional biometric authentication for App access No (user choice)

All permissions are "normal" permissions granted automatically at install time. No runtime permission prompts are required.

9. Children's Privacy

10. Data Retention

All data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it. There is no automatic deletion and no server-side retention. You are the sole controller of your data and its lifecycle.
